What Is Dashlane?

Dashlane is a leading password manager and digital security platform trusted by millions of individuals and businesses worldwide. Designed to simplify and strengthen your online security, Dashlane securely stores all your passwords, payment details, personal information, and sensitive credentials in one encrypted vault. Rather than relying on memory or unsafe habits like reusing passwords, Dashlane generates strong unique passwords and fills them in automatically whenever you need them.

Beyond password management, Dashlane also offers a built-in VPN for secure browsing, dark web monitoring to alert you if your data has been compromised, and passkey support for the latest passwordless authentication standards. Your Dashlane account is the gateway to all of these protections — making the login process both important and worth understanding thoroughly.

How to Login Dashlane on Multiple Devices

Dashlane offers a smooth and consistent login experience across all major platforms. Here's how to access your account on each device type.

🖥️ On a Web Browser (Computer / Laptop)
  1. Open your preferred web browser on your computer.
  2. Go to the official Dashlane web application in the browser's address bar.
  3. Click the Log In button on the Dashlane homepage.
  4. Enter the email address associated with your Dashlane account and click Continue.
  5. Enter your Master Password in the provided field — this is the single password you created when setting up Dashlane.
  6. Complete any two-factor authentication prompt if enabled on your account.
  7. Click Log In to enter your secure vault and access all saved credentials.
🧩 Via Browser Extension (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
  1. Install the Dashlane browser extension from your browser's official extension or add-on store.
  2. Once installed, click the Dashlane icon that appears in your browser toolbar.
  3. Select Log In from the extension popup panel.
  4. Enter your registered email address and proceed to the next step.
  5. Type your Master Password and confirm any two-factor authentication if prompted.
  6. The extension will now be active, enabling autofill and password management on all websites you visit.
📱 On a Smartphone or Tablet (iOS & Android)
  1. Download the official Dashlane app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. Open the app once installation is complete.
  3. Tap Log In on the welcome screen.
  4. Enter your registered email address and tap Continue.
  5. Enter your Master Password to unlock your vault.
  6. Complete the two-factor authentication step if it has been activated on your account.
  7. Enable biometric login such as Face ID or fingerprint recognition for faster future access when prompted.
💻 On the Dashlane Desktop App (Windows & macOS)
  1. Download and install the Dashlane desktop application for your operating system.
  2. Launch the application from your applications folder or start menu.
  3. Enter your Dashlane account email address on the login screen.
  4. Input your Master Password and complete two-factor verification if required.
  5. Your vault will sync automatically, giving you full offline and online access to your saved data.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Encountering problems while logging in can be frustrating, especially with a security tool. Here are the most common Dashlane login issues and how to resolve them effectively:

  • Forgotten Master Password: Dashlane uses a zero-knowledge model, meaning it cannot recover your Master Password for you. If you have account recovery options set up — such as a recovery key or biometric backup — use those. Otherwise, you may need to reset your account, which will clear your vault data, so always keep your recovery key stored safely.
  • Incorrect Email Address: Ensure you are using the exact email address tied to your Dashlane account. Check for subtle typos or confirm whether you registered with a work or personal address.
  • Two-Factor Authentication Code Not Arriving: Verify that your authenticator app is synchronized to the correct time, or check that your registered phone number is active. If using an authenticator app, ensure the time on your device is set to automatic.
  • Browser Extension Not Responding: Disable and re-enable the Dashlane extension through your browser's extension settings. If the issue persists, remove and reinstall the extension from the official store.
  • App Crashing on Mobile: Update the Dashlane app to its latest version through the App Store or Play Store. If problems continue, uninstall and reinstall the app — your data is securely stored in the cloud and will restore upon login.
  • Account Locked Due to Multiple Failed Attempts: Dashlane may temporarily restrict access after several incorrect password attempts. Wait a few minutes before trying again and ensure Caps Lock is not active when entering your Master Password.
  • Sync Issues Across Devices: If your saved passwords are not appearing on a newly logged-in device, ensure you have an active internet connection and check that your subscription plan includes multi-device sync, as this feature may vary by plan tier.
